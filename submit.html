How to Submit a Letter

Submit your letter to Congress through this form. Your submission will be reviewed and, if approved, published on this site.

  • Write your letter in the editor below
  • Use the toolbar buttons to format your text (bold, italic, lists, etc.)
  • Provide a clear title and the date of your letter
  • Click "Submit Letter" to create a pull request on GitHub
  • Your letter will be reviewed before being published
Formatting Tips: Use **text** for bold, *text* for italic, and - item for bullet lists. Or use the toolbar buttons above.
